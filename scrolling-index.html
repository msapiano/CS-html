The CLAS Experience

Our faculty and staff want you to have an enriching experience in and out of the classroom. Want to intern at a local company or study abroad in a cosmopolitan city like London or Berlin? Take a field trip with classmates to Washington, D.C. or work one-on-one on a research project with your professor? You will have the chance to do all this and more. Hear what our students say about the many exciting adventures they had during their CLAS experience.

Meaningful Careers

We are proud that our graduates have found fulfilling careers throughout the Michiana region and beyond. Whether they work in environmental sustainability, the legal profession, or social services, CLAS majors make a difference when they graduate. We are proud of them all. Are you a CLAS alum? Tell us what you’ve been up to since graduation!
