Tips to succeed in College

  • e.g., I am not getting the concept, yet!, but I will work hard and seek the needed help to succeed
  • Spaced Retrieval (quiz, reflection, using greater effort), Interleaving topics, Making connections to previously understood concepts, Varied practice
  • Have a Growth Mindset!
  • Mastery! should be the goal
  • Practice, Practice, Practice, Practice, ...
  • Ask for help!
  • Learn how to take notes during lectures (or while you are studying) effectively!
  • Learn how to take notes effectively, but after comprehending the material!
  • Do not underestimate the time required to complete assignments, studying for exams, etc.
  • Start Early!
