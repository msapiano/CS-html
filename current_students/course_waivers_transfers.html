  • You have the option to test-out from world language requirement by contacting the World Languages department.
  • Your requirement will be waived if you have taken a single foreign language for 3 years (6 semesters) or more in high school and received a grade of C or better. Meet with your academic advisor with your high-school transcript.
  • If you are a native speaker of a language other than English, then contact the college Dean's Office in Wiekamp Building 3rd floor. Also consult with your academic advisor and World Languages department. You will be asked to demonstrate the proficency in your native language.

Have you completed AP Computer Science A?

  • If you got score 3, 4, or 5 your course will transfer as CSCI-A 201 Introduction to Programming I 

Have you completed AP Computer Science Principles?

  • If you got score 3 your course will transfer as CSCI-UNDI Computer Science Undistributed (3 credits)
  • If you got score 4 or 5 your course will transfer as CSCI-B 100 Problem Solving using Computers
