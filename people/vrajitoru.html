Dana Vrajitoru

Title: Student Club Advisor and Associate Professor

Office: NS 337

Phone: (574)520-4525

Email:dvrajito@iusb.edu

Web Page: http://www.cs.iusb.edu/~dvrajito/

Education

  • 1997 – Doctor of Science – University of Neuchâtel, Switzerland

Brief Biography

She grew up in Romania where she started her undergraduate studies at the University “Al. I. Cuza”, Iasi. As a teenager, she had a chance to spend a few months in Morocco where her father had a teaching job, and attended a local Spanish school. While in college in Iasi, she also studied singing and guitar at the People’s School of Arts. In 1991 she moved to Neuchâtel Switzerland, where she first acquired a second undergraduate diploma, and then started graduate studies. As a graduate student she was also a motorcycle enthusiast, which later was an inspiration for some of her research projects.

Achievements 

One of her papers, “Shared Memory Genetic Algorithms in a Multi-Agent Context” was nominated for the Best Paper award in the category Parallel Evolutionary Systems at the Genetic and Evolutionary Computation Conference in 2010.

Research Interests 

Research interests: genetic algorithms; intelligent systems; simulation and modeling; parallel computation; information retrieval; artificial intelligence.
