Computer Science Adjunct Faculty


The Department of Computer and Information Sciences frequently has one or more adjunct faculty positions available every semester. Persons with excellent teaching or industrial qualifications in computer science are encouraged to apply. Salary will be commensurate with experience.

IU South Bend is one of eight campuses of Indiana University. The full-time faculty of the department includes ten computer scientists and additional associate faculty members. IU South Bend is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action employer; women and minority candidates are encouraged to apply.

Applications are accepted throughout the year. If possible, recommendation letters should comment on teaching.

Send vita and list of references to the Chair of the Department of Computer and Information Sciences.
